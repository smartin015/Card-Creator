  • Welcome!

    We've prepared a brief tutorial on the basics of combat.

    After the tutorial, you'll go through a practice encounter to get comfortable with the mechanics.

  • Manging Enemies

    The arrows at the bottom of the screen track how many enemies you're fighting of each Threat level.

    Before starting each round of combat, make sure to update these counters.

  • Combat Rounds

    To begin a round of combat, tap the center of the screen.

    Tap the screen again when everyone has played their abilities.

    Remember that you take more damage the longer you take to act!

  • Surges

    Surges are powerful attacks, with special effects listed on the enemy's card.

    When a surge happens, resolves surges for ALL enemies at that tier.
