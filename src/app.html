  • Welcome!

    We've prepared a brief tutorial on the basics of combat.

    After the tutorial, you'll go through a sample encounter to practice the mechanics.

  • Manging Enemies

    The arrows at the bottom of the screen track how many enemies you're fighting of each Threat level.

    Before starting each round of combat, make sure to update these counters.

  • Combat Rounds

    To begin a round of combat, tap the center of the screen.

    Tap the screen again when everyone has played their abilities.

    Remember that the longer you take, the more damage you take!

  • Resolving Combat

    Once the round has ended, everyone selects their ability's target(s).

    Then, all players roll for their abilities and role-play the results.

    Finally, enemy damage is dealt to the party.

  • Surges

    Surges are powerful attacks, with special effects listed on the enemy's card.

    When a surge happens, resolves surges for ALL enemies at that tier, before resolving abilities.

  • Encounter Time

    That's it - you're ready for your first encounter!

    Draw a tier 1 enemy, and let's practice your combat skills.
